# Product Overview

The platform combines a user-facing property marketplace with powerful backend automation workflows to enhance the real estate sales process.

## Value Proposition
- **Automated Lead Management**: Capture, qualify, and route leads automatically using n8n workflows
- **Intelligent Customer Engagement**: AI-powered chatbot integration via Chatwoot for 24/7 customer support
- **Multi-Portal Synchronization**: Seamlessly sync property listings across multiple real estate portals
- **Data-Driven Insights**: Analytics and reporting to track property performance and lead conversion
- **Streamlined Operations**: Automated visit scheduling, follow-ups, and document management

## Key Features

### Property Management
- Comprehensive property listing system with images, features, and location data
- Advanced search and filtering capabilities
- Property favorites and saved searches for users
- View tracking and analytics per property
- Multi-currency support (default CLP)

### Lead Capture & Qualification
- Automated lead capture from multiple sources
- Lead scoring and qualification system
- Intelligent lead routing to appropriate agents
- Source tracking and attribution

### Customer Engagement
- Chatwoot integration for live chat and AI chatbot
- Automated property alerts based on saved searches
- Visit scheduling and management system
- Automated follow-up sequences

### Automation Workflows (n8n)
1. Lead capture and qualification
2. Property alert system
3. Automated follow-ups
4. Visit management
5. Multi-portal synchronization
6. AI chatbot integration
7. Analytics and reporting
8. Document management
9. Marketing automation
10. CRM integration

### Agent Management
- Agent profiles with contact information and bio
- Property assignment to agents
- Visit and lead assignment
- Performance tracking

## Target Users

### Primary Users
- **Real Estate Agents**: Manage properties, leads, and client interactions
- **Property Buyers/Renters**: Search properties, schedule visits, save favorites
- **Real Estate Agencies**: Oversee operations, analytics, and team performance

### Use Cases
1. **Property Search**: Users browse and filter properties by location, price, type, and features
2. **Lead Generation**: Capture interested buyers through contact forms and chatbot interactions
3. **Automated Qualification**: Score and route leads based on engagement and criteria
4. **Visit Scheduling**: Automated booking system for property viewings
5. **Property Alerts**: Notify users when properties matching their criteria become available
6. **Multi-Channel Marketing**: Sync listings across portals and manage campaigns
7. **Performance Analytics**: Track property views, lead conversion, and agent performance
